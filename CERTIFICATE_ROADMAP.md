## Portfolio Completion Criteria

The repo becomes portfolio-ready when it includes:

- At least one runnable Bedrock API lab.
- Clean setup instructions.
- No credentials or secrets.
- A short responsible AI checklist.
- Notes that explain business relevance.
- Screenshots or sanitized sample outputs, if available.
- A clear progress tracker that distinguishes completed from in-progress items.
